For Candidates

Work in Germany — a real contract, no placement fee.

Are you a tradesperson, technician or plant mechanic from the EU looking for work in Germany? We hire you directly at Wellwork Germany GmbH and place you with our industrial and construction clients. Your wages are paid in euros, on time, every month — and we take care of the relocation.

Your benefits

What you get with us

A permanent employment contract

You are hired directly by Wellwork Germany GmbH — not by a subcontractor, and not as a bogus self-employed worker. All your social security contributions are covered.

Paid in euros, on time

Monthly payslip in euros, paid by the 10th of the following month. No piecework, no cash-in-hand, no tricks — a standard German employment contract.

Housing organised for you

We arrange accommodation close to the site: clean, furnished and affordable. Transport between accommodation and workplace is taken care of too.

Registration and insurance

We support you with your official registration in Germany, statutory health insurance and all the paperwork that comes with working here as an EU employee.

No placement fee

You pay us nothing. No commission, no upfront cost, no placement charge. Our clients pay us — you just get the job.

A contact person on the ground

If anything is off — on site or in daily life — you have one fixed contact who speaks German, English and, wherever possible, your mother tongue.

What we expect from you

Requirements for our tradespeople

Professional qualifications

  • Completed vocational training in the relevant trade
  • At least two years of hands-on experience (more for some positions)
  • Confident handling of the trade's standard tools, and genuine site experience
  • Physically fit for the advertised work

General requirements

  • EU citizenship, or a valid EU work permit
  • English at site level — German is a plus, not a must
  • EU driving licence (Class B) — required for many positions
  • Willingness to live and work in Germany for several months
The application process

From first application to your first day on site

Usually three to four weeks from contract signature, depending on how quickly we can align your documents and the right site.

Send in your application

CV (with certificates) and a short note on your qualifications, by email.

Video interview

A 30-minute call with our team: technical questions, expectations, preferred locations.

Written contract offer

You receive a German-law employment contract setting out all terms in writing.

Travel and deployment

We arrange your trip, your housing and your German registration. Then you start on site.

Ready for the next step?

Send us your CV and we will come back to you within three working days with an initial assessment.
