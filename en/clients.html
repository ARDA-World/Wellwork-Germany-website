How we work together

Two clear models — no gimmicks

Project supply

You have a clearly defined project — a construction job, a turnaround, a seasonal peak — and you need extra skilled hands for a limited time. We deploy the team for the full duration of the project.

  • Typical duration: three to eighteen months
  • We arrange travel, housing and rotation planning
  • Billed against actual hours worked

Long-term reinforcement

You need tradespeople the open market simply isn't delivering. We hire them, you direct them technically — up to the eighteen-month assignment cap under the AÜG. After that, a direct-hire handover to your company is the norm.

  • Assignments running over multiple months
  • Take-over by your company after eighteen months — common and straightforward
  • A dedicated account manager handles the ongoing administration
What you can expect

Our service promise

Pre-qualified profiles

Every candidate is screened for qualifications, German or English ability, references, driving licence and willingness to work on site in Germany. You don't get a stack of CVs — you get three to five genuinely matched profiles.

Housing and transport

We arrange shared or individual accommodation close to the site, organise transport and handle the official registration with the German authorities.

Straightforward billing

A monthly invoice with hours per tradesperson, breaks and holiday surcharges, all clearly itemised. Payment term: fourteen days. Net hourly rate from €28/hour (standard) to €32/hour (specialist).

A single point of contact

You reach a person, not a ticket queue. Operational questions, timesheet approvals, extensions — everything goes through one hand.

Safety and compliance

All tradespeople are covered by accident insurance, properly registered for social security and have completed the required safety briefings before starting on site.

No upfront fees

You only pay for what we deliver. No search fee, no retainer, no package rate. Everything is billed by the hour — so our incentive is straightforward: actually deliver.

You need tradespeople — and ideally this week?

Give us a call. Twenty minutes is usually enough to scope what you need. By the next business day you get a written summary: what we can supply, by when and at what rate.
