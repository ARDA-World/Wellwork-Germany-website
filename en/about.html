About Wellwork Germany

A staffing firm that actually delivers.

We are not a headhunter, not an aggregator and not a platform. We are a licensed staffing provider based in Löbau, Saxony, set up specifically to supply EU tradespeople to German industrial and construction clients who need the job done — on site, on the day agreed.

Our story

Built out of operational need.

Wellwork Germany GmbH is part of the Wellwork Group, which has run staffing operations in Slovakia and Hungary for years. The German entity was set up in 2024, after a major international client — Centerra/Constellis — needed tradespeople for several German sites and no agency on the market could deliver reliably.

Today we have 25 tradespeople on assignment across four German locations, and we are steadily expanding into the wider Mittelstand. Our benchmark is simple: a staffing provider you can actually call, where someone actually picks up.

What matters to us

Three principles, not ten.

  • We deliver. If we commit to a date, we meet it. If we can't deliver, we say so before you sign — not afterwards.
  • Transparent pricing. One hourly rate per skill level, monthly invoicing, no hidden fees. You know at the start of the month what you pay at the end of it.
  • A single point of contact. Every client gets a dedicated account manager. Phone, email or Teams — never a ticket queue.
Regulated and compliant

AÜG licence and compliance

Temporary labour supply is strictly regulated in Germany. We have held an unlimited AÜG licence, issued by the Federal Employment Agency in Kiel, since 10 March 2025 — the foundation for any serious client relationship.

Unlimited AÜG licence

Issued by the Federal Employment Agency, Kiel. Valid since 10 March 2025. Annual reporting obligation to the Bundesagentur für Arbeit.

Equal pay and equal treatment

We apply the AÜG rules on equal pay and equal treatment in full once the statutory assignment period is reached.

GDPR-compliant

All candidate data is processed in line with GDPR. Data processing agreements (AVV) are available on request.
